BUILD ON A FLAT SURFACE

Build the frame on a glass or granite surface
to ensure you can get it as square as possible. Using something like 123 blocks
or 90 degree squares may help.

Put the parts together, make sure to tighten
the M5x16 BHCS firmly, checking that it’s square.
